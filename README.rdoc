Watch1t
-------
Watch1t is a web application made for people who want fast and reliable information about their favourite TV series. 
-------
The feautures we are planning to implement are:
==> A vast database of series produced nowdays.
==> Fast synchronization with IMDB.
==> Simple and user friendly system for disscussion.
-------
The only thing you need to do is to register and confirm your email address.
